About

CookieCaptain is a rising independent YouTube channel that creates straightforward videos where you can find best methods to type faster and more accurately with every practice session and get familiar with the basics of mechanical keyboards such as how they work and how you can build your own custom keyboard.

Progress with Every Keypress

With everyone using computers nowadays, whether it be for school, work, or daily tasks, being able to type quickly is a very important skill to spend less time writing text and enhance your productivity and efficiency, and there are a number of abilities you need to master to achieve this. As a typist that reaches over 200 WPM and gained 170+ WPM by practicing for 3 years, my goal is to teach as many people as possible to develop this skill by practicing the right way so that they improve with every key they press.

Getting Started with Mechanical Keyboards

The keyboard that you are using is also a very important factor that affects how you type, and using one that you're comfortable with will greatly improve your typing skills. Building a mechanical keyboard for yourself offers a number of benefits and allows you to precisely meet all of your preferences such as the typing feel and sound as you can pick out every part for yourself. With my guides and reviews, this channel will make it a lot easier to understand the basics of mechanical keyboards and decide if you should get one, as well as assist you through choosing and building your own custom keyboard and recognizing what to consider.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best way to improve my typing speed?

If you want to really increase your typing speed, the only thing that I can say is to keep on practicing and always be mindful of how you're doing it so that you develop the right muscle memory to type efficiently and accurately.

How can I increase my typing accuracy?

To improve your accuracy, don't rush when you're typing and always maintain a mindset that focuses on the keys you're pressing so that you know you're hitting the right ones. That way, you won't have to sacrifice any of your accuracy to gain speed.

What are the benefits of using a mechanical keyboard?

There are many benefits to using a mechanical keyboard, such as their durability, responsiveness, aesthetics, typing feel and sound, and customizability, enhancing productivity and efficiency for users typing on a keyboard with maximum comfort.

How do I choose the right mechanical keyboard for me?

To find the right mechanical keyboard that suits your needs and preferences, there are a number of factors you'll want to look at in each part, such as their shape, material, mechanism, and many more properties. If you want all of these to be exactly how you want it, I recommend buiding your own custom mechanical keyboard which I'll have a guide for in the future.

I decided that I also want to use my skills and knowledge to teach others who are interested in getting started in this field.
